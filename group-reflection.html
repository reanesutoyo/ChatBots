Group Reflection

Justin Healy

What our group has done well so far has been our ability to stick to our deadlines. During our meetings we have specified which work was needed to be completed and when. So far, we have all stuck to these deadlines and have been able to offer our pieces of work to the group for absorption into the collective.

What could be improved in our team, is our ability to schedule meetings. Most of our meetings have been organised the day of, or the day before. We could improve by having a more structured meeting system.

The thing that has surprised me about this experience is the same thing I have I learnt. It is that it takes time to settle into a groove with a group, even when all communication is online. I expected this part to be much easier as an online experience, but it is essentially how it would be if we were physically in a classroom together. We all had to find our way of contributing our parts and find our place in the team.

In the way we structured GitHub, we used teams to host and edit all our sections, then when completed, these could be pushed to GitHub for web hosting.

James Stathakis

For Group #14, Assessment task 2, the group was amazing. Everyone in the group was kind and supportive, and there wasn’t a single malicious or hostile member of the group. The group came together and welcomed each member of the group extremely well and the group divided up the workpretty well. However, from personal experience in the group, I didn’t do much, if any. The work I was assigned, I was doing the IT work interviews, however, instead of interviewing an IT Professional personally, I took various interviews of different kinds of IT Professionals over the last 20 odd years and was going to compare them. I was doing relatively well in this when a group member reached out in the group stating he’d done the interview. After a few days of lack of communication on my part to the rest of the group, I returned to see that the group was ready to cut me out of the Assessment.There’reno hard feelings towards this, I had done little to the group. However, upon my return, there was a conscious effort on the group’s part to include me back into the group, and tohelp outwith anything to get my contribution up, which was surprising, and comforting. I’m not a very sociable individual, but to find such a warm and welcoming group of people for this assessment, was good.

Alice w lin

In general, I enjoy group work, and although a lot of unexpected events occurred with missing team members, and us unable to have meetings according to everyone’s time schedules etc.; it was still very enjoyable. The great thing about teamwork is that you can share your ideas, your questions, and your concerns with what you are doing with the others in the team, ultimately working together to achieve a common goal, that is to finish the assignment as best as we can!

Another aspect I think our team did very well is that we split the sections among each member in the start very carefully before we started doing anything. From my experience, no matter if this is related work or study, it is important to trust your team, and you must assign each section properly to avoid unnecessary disagreements. We tried our best at assigning the sections equally, although it didn’t really work as we intended, and there are some that might have done more work than others, there was no conflict. I think Everyone tried very hard, and everyone in our team is extremely bright and hardworking, everyone’s got their own ideas and opinions, everyone had their skills to share with everyone else, making this experience particular enjoyable. Overall, the general experience for assignment 2 is very well enjoyed, and I would do another assignment with everyone in no time.
